How I Work

Every project starts with understanding the why. What business problem are we solving? What invariants must hold? What are the real constraints?

I practice Domain-Driven Design religiously. Business rules live in the domain layer—nowhere else. The code should read like the spec.

I believe in fail-fast, no-fallback approaches. Explicit errors beat silent failures. Strict typing beats Any. Clear contracts beat implicit assumptions.

And I document everything with AGENTS.md files—architectural guides that help both humans and AI assistants understand the codebase rules.
